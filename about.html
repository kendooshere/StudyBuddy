About Us.

StudyBuddy was created as a means for our fellow BCA students to get easier access to resources like Notes, Previous year questions and YouTube playlists tailored according to different semesters and their subjects. Students can increase their productivity and focus using our StudyRoom so that they can cover more in less time. With very simple and mobile friendly UI, we are trying to make it easy for students to study, and enjoy while doing it.

Features.

StudyBuddy is packed full of features that will elevate the quality of learning and preparing for exams.

Notes, Previous year questions and YouTube playlists tailored according to syllabus.

Pomodoro Timer for increased productivity.

Lofi-Music Player for increased Focus.
